Khand
Gold Reserve

A delicate, unrefined cane sugar. Fine as sand, rich with the memory of the cane stalk. This is sweetness in its most elegant natural form.

Add to Cart Save to Wishlist
Ingredients 100% Raw Sugarcane Juice. Nothing else.
Origin Cold-pressed in the fields of Uttar Pradesh.
Pairs With Light roast coffee, shortbread, cardamom chai.
The Ritual Dissolve one teaspoon in warm espresso. Listen to the silence.

The Morning Ritual

Because of its fine texture, Khand dissolves instantly into hot beverages. When stirred into a fresh espresso or a robust black tea, it imparts a subtle note of caramel without overpowering the roast. Close your eyes. Taste the difference.
